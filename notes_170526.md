## Session B

- In John 15:10 Jesus said as he has kept his Father's commandments, he remains in his heart.
- The heart of the commandment is love.
- If anyone is breaking the commandment, love is lacking in their life.
- Without keeping the commandments, we cannot grow in love.
- Most of the people, see the commandments as a burden.
- The commandments are not burdensome, the word of God says this.
- It is essential to have love of God in life, but people see it as a burden, then we don't want to hear it.
- The foundation is God's mercy hence we need to believe and trust.
- Everything in the universe can be substituted, and every situation can be redeemed if we trust in the love and mercy of God.
- Only when we love Jesus we are in true love. When we love Jesus, we grow in love.
- If you learn only to love yourself without loving Jesus, then you're growing in selfishness.
- Wisdom and knowledge prayers for 15 minutes everyday is important.
- Start 15 minutes personal prayer invite holy spirit and ask for wisdom and knowledge.
- The Eucharist should become the heart beat and not cause difference in spirit.
- Altar, Chalice, and Patent (ACP) is supposed to be the heartbeat of the mass which should be our heartbeat.
